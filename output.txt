Added backend support for session sorting.

GET /support/sessions now accepts:

sortBy=startedAt
sortBy=initial_date
sortBy=updatedAt
sortBy=last_interaction
and:

sortDirection=desc
sortDirection=asc
So the frontend can call, for example:

/support/sessions?product=merxus&sortBy=startedAt&sortDirection=desc
or:

/support/sessions?product=merxus&sortBy=last_interaction&sortDirection=desc
